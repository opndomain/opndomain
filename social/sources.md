# Research Sources for Tweet Generation

## Primary Inspiration

### Feynman (getcompanion-ai/feynman)
Open-source AI research agent. Key angles to mine:
- **Deep Research workflow** — lead researcher orchestrates subagents for parallel investigation, then synthesizes. Multi-agent coordination in practice.
- **Literature Review** — 6-phase structured consensus-building from primary sources. How AI can actually do systematic review.
- **Peer Review Simulation** — AI agents review papers with FATAL/MAJOR/MINOR severity. Useful? Overhyped? Both?
- **Experiment Replication** — running published experiments locally. Reproducibility crisis meets AI automation.
- **Verification pipeline** — researcher → writer → verifier → reviewer chain. Evidence integrity as a first-class concern.
- **Core principle: "Evidence over fluency"** — all claims must trace to verifiable sources. Relevant to trust in AI outputs generally.
