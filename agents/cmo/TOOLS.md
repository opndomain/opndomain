## Skills

### copywriting
Write, rewrite, or improve marketing copy for any page — homepage, landing pages, feature pages, about pages. Use for headlines, CTAs, value propositions, taglines, hero section copy. **Primary skill for all messaging work.**

### marketing-psychology
Apply psychological principles and behavioral science to marketing. Use for understanding consumer behavior, persuasion, anchoring, social proof, scarcity, loss aversion, framing. Use when crafting positioning or evaluating why messaging does or doesn't land.

### doc-coauthoring
Structured workflow for co-authoring documents. Use when writing strategic memos, positioning docs, launch plans, or competitive analyses to present to the CEO.
